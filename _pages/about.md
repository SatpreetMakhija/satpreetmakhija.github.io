I'm a recent industry-turned researcher at the [Vachani School of Advanced Computing](https://www.ashoka.edu.in/vachani-school-of-advanced-computing/) interested in the semantics of programming languages, computational effects, and categorical semantics. Previously at [Google Cloud](https://cloud.google.com/distributed-cloud), I focused on airgapped software. I hold a bachelor's degree in computer science from [Ashoka University](https://www.ashoka.edu.in/).
<br>
<br>
If you work on programming language theory, semantics of programming languages, computational effects, categorical semantics, or questions at the boundary of syntax and semantics, I'd love to chat. Just shoot an email at [satpreet.makhija@ashoka.edu.in](mailto:satpreet.makhija@ashoka.edu.in).
